Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

CocoaLana AutoPublish ("we", "our", or "us") is a desktop application that helps fashion e-commerce sellers publish product images to social media platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, X/Twitter, TikTok) to CocoaLana AutoPublish, we collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Account information: Platform usernames, display names, and user IDs
  • Authentication tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to interact with platform APIs
  • Content metadata: Post IDs, timestamps, captions, and engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, views) for content you publish through our app
  • Media files: Images you upload to the app for publishing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Content management: Post images and content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Performance insights: Retrieve and display engagement data (likes, comments, views) for your published content to help you make informed decisions
  • Account management: Display your connected accounts and manage authentication status
  • Platform integration: Read your Pinterest boards, Facebook pages, and other platform structures to enable targeted content distribution

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. We do not operate any external servers and do not transmit your personal data to any server controlled by us. Your OAuth tokens are stored locally and are used exclusively to communicate directly with the respective social media platform APIs (Meta Graph API, Pinterest API, X API, TikTok API).

4. Third-Party Services

CocoaLana AutoPublish interacts with the following third-party APIs on your behalf when you authorize access:

  • Pinterest API (v5) — To read your boards, create Pins, and retrieve Pin analytics
  • Meta Graph API — To publish to Instagram and Facebook, and retrieve engagement data
  • X (Twitter) API (v2) — To publish tweets with images
  • TikTok Content API — To publish content to TikTok

We do not share your data with any third parties beyond the direct API interactions you authorize. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data on their servers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained locally on your device for as long as you use the application. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Removing individual accounts from the app (deletes associated tokens and data)
  • Using the app's backup/restore feature to export or clear data
  • Uninstalling the application (removes all local data)

6. Children's Privacy

CocoaLana AutoPublish is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
